Happy New Year,
Princess Laiza

The clock strikes twelve, a new year dawns,
But time feels different, not the same as before.
For twenty-twenty-three, a treasure born,
You blossomed into mine, and closed my love's door.

We met and mingled, souls began to spark,
Laughter's music filling every empty ark.
Conversations deep, like whispered dreams,
And stolen glances painted moonlight beams.

From awkward dates to silly walks hand-in-hand,
From whispered secrets to adventures planned,
You've built a nest within my heart's domain,
And filled it with a love that won't wane.

Oh, darling girl, with eyes that shine so bright,
Your smile, a sunrise chasing winter's night.
My gratitude unfolds, a paper flower,
Each petal blooms with every happy hour.

So let this new year echo with my vow,
To love you fiercely, here and somehow,
Forever grateful for the day you came,
My love, my girl, I meow you all the same.

-Forever yours, Chintu 🐱
